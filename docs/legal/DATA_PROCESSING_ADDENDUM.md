# Data Processing Addendum

Last updated: March 12, 2026

This Data Processing Addendum applies when Recete Retention Agent processes personal data on behalf of merchants using the service.

## Roles

- Merchant: data controller for customer personal data uploaded to or synced into the service
- Recete: data processor acting on the merchant's documented instructions

## Subject Matter and Purpose

Recete processes customer personal data only to provide the contracted service, including:

- post-purchase customer support workflows
- consent-aware WhatsApp messaging
- conversation history and merchant reply tools
- product guidance and analytics requested by the merchant
- GDPR export, deletion, and consent-management flows

## Categories of Data

- customer name
- customer phone number
- order and delivery context
- consent status
- conversation content

## Processor Commitments

Recete will:

- process personal data only for the purposes described in merchant-facing documentation and instructions
- implement encryption in transit and encryption at rest for sensitive customer data
- maintain tenant isolation controls
- restrict internal access to authorized personnel with a legitimate business need
- support merchant requests for export, deletion, and consent updates
- notify merchants without undue delay after confirming a security incident affecting personal data

## Retention

Recete retains personal data only as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, and honor merchant or data subject requests. Merchant deletion requests trigger soft deletion and scheduled permanent deletion workflows.

## Subprocessors

Recete may use subprocessors that are necessary to operate the service, including hosting, database, authentication, queueing, AI, and messaging providers.

## Incorporation

This Addendum is incorporated into the merchant agreement and Terms of Service for merchants using Recete Retention Agent.
