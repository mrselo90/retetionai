## Installation Instructions

### For Merchants

1. Click "Install" on the Recete Retention Agent app page
2. Authorize the app to access your Shopify store
3. Complete the setup wizard:
   - Connect your WhatsApp Business API credentials
   - Import your products (automatic or manual)
   - Configure your AI assistant's personality
4. Start receiving customer conversations via WhatsApp!

### Technical Requirements

- Shopify store (any plan)
- WhatsApp Business API account (or use our test mode)
- Products with URLs or descriptions
